12 data-columns responsive

Example:

  • Desktop (default): 12 data-columns
  • Tablet landscape (md): 4 data-columns
  • Tablet portrait / Mobile landscape (sm): 2 data-columns
  • Mobile portrait (xs): 1 data-column

Note: Setting data-columns to 0, md0, sm0 or xs0 will hide the data-column for that breakpoint.
